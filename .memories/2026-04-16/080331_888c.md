## Brief Repositioning Design

### WHAT
Added a new design document at `docs/plans/2026-04-16-brief-repositioning-design.md` that reframes Goldfish's current `plan` artifact as a `brief`.

### WHY
Native harness planning now overlaps with Goldfish's existing plan tool and hook behavior. Auto-mirroring harness plans into Goldfish creates duplicate sources of truth and stale execution detail.

### HOW
- Chose `brief` as the replacement name over `initiative`
- Defined the brief as a compact strategic document, not an execution plan
- Specified product changes across tool naming, hook behavior, recall wording, checkpoint affinity, skills, storage, and migration
- Kept the migration strategy backward-compatible and lazy rather than requiring a one-off conversion

### IMPACT
- Gives Goldfish a clearer niche: durable strategic context plus evidence trail
- Removes the main source of toe-stepping with harness-native planning
- Creates a concrete spec for future implementation work instead of leaving the decision as hand-wavy product talk
