## Task 2: Serialize planId in checkpoint frontmatter

### What
Added `planId` serialization to `formatCheckpoint()` and deserialization to `parseCheckpointFile()` in `src/checkpoints.ts`.

### Why
Part of plan-checkpoint affinity feature. Checkpoints need to persist their plan association in the markdown frontmatter so it survives save/load cycles.

### How
- `formatCheckpoint`: Added `if (checkpoint.planId)` block after `summary` to include `planId` in the YAML frontmatter object
- `parseCheckpointFile`: Added `if (frontmatter.planId)` line after `summary` parsing to extract and stringify the field
- Both omit the field entirely when not present (no noise in existing checkpoints)

### Tests (5 new)
1. `includes planId in frontmatter when present` (formatCheckpoint)
2. `omits planId from frontmatter when not present` (formatCheckpoint)
3. `parses planId from frontmatter` (parseCheckpointFile)
4. `omits planId when not in frontmatter` (parseCheckpointFile)
5. `roundtrips planId through format/parse` (parseCheckpointFile)
